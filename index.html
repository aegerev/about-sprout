Your Success Is Our Mission. We Exist Thanks To You.

Sprout is all about one thing: making sure that your child succeeds. As the number one educational media brand for kids, Sprout helps people of any age, gender, or race to succeed in life. That is our mission. Through curriculum-based media and community-based programs, Sprout wants people to see themselves uniquely reflected and celebrated in lovable, diverse characters who serve as positive role models. Sprout also want people - be it kids or adults - to explore their feelings and discover new adventures along the way.
